After all that designing, you deserve a break! Try these quick exercises to refresh:
Eye Reset
Use the 20-20-20 rule—look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes.
Hand Stretches
Open and close your fists slowly, or try wrist rolls to relieve tension from repetitive design work.
Color Reset
Look at neutral colors or soft pastels to give your eyes a break from bright and saturated hues.
Stretch Freely
Grab a piece of paper and doodle with no design goal—just let your mind wander creatively.
