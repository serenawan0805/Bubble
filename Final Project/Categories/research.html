After all that research, you deserve a break! Try these quick exercises to refresh:
Mental Mapping
Step back and sketch a quick mind map of your research to refocus your thoughts.
Window Gaze
Look out a window and observe your surroundings for a few minutes to reduce mental fatigue.
Body Movement
Walk around the room or do light stretching to combat physical inactivity.
Hydration & Snack
Enjoy a light, healthy snack like nuts or fruit while stepping away from the screen.
