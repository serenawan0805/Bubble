After all that education, you deserve a break! Try these quick exercises to refresh:
Reflection Exercise
Write down 3 things you’ve learned so far and why they’re meaningful.
Mindful Reading
Take a moment to read a non-academic book or poem to shift mental focus.
Body Stretching
Perform neck stretches and shoulder rolls to release built-up tension.
Breathing Practice
Try the 4-7-8 breathing technique to refocus and calm your mind.
