After all that social, you deserve a break! Try these quick exercises to refresh:
Gratitude Exercise
Write down 3 things or people you’re thankful for in your social network.
Quiet Time
Spend 5 minutes in silence to reset from social interactions.
Eye Care
Rub your palms together, then place them gently over your closed eyes for 20 seconds.
Breathing Exercise
Focus on deep breathing to calm overstimulated nerves after a long chat session.
